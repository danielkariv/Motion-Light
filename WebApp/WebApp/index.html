Motion Light

Motion Light is a multiplayer game inspired by the game Snake and the movie TRON.
The goal is to block the other player, and be the last one to survive.
You are controlling your bike using the Arrows keys in the keyboard ( ↑ ↓ ← →).
The blue bike is the player who openned the server, and the red bike is the player who joined.
